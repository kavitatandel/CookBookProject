Butter Paneer Masala

Description

Butter paneer masala is one of the most popular dish for lunch or dinner. It is made of paneer along with other indian spices.

Ingredients

Paneer:

250 gm

Onions:

2 medium (cut into pieces)

Tomatoes:

3 medium (cut into pieces)

Ginger garlic paste:

1 tsp

Whole Cinamon:

1 nos.

Tej Patta:

1 nos.

Salt:

Salt: as per taste

Turemeric powder:

1/2 tsp

Coriander powder:

1 tablespoon

Sugar:

1/2 tsp

Red chilli powder:

1 tbsp

Garam masala:

1/8 tsp

Coriander:

1 tbsp for garnishing

Cashew:

5-6 nos.

Jogurt:

4 tbsp

Oil:

1/2 tbsp

Butter:

1/2 tbsp

Method

1. Cut Paneer into small pieces and fry or shallow fry them until light golden brown.

2. In a pan add chopped onions, tomatoes, cashews and cook until becomes soft. Put it aside to become cold and grind it to paste.

3. In a pan, add oil and butter. Add Tej patta, cinamon and fry it for 5 seconds. Now on a medium flame, add paste and all other spices, salt and cook it till oil comes out.

4. Now add jogurt and mix well everything. Add water to adjust consistency.

5. Add paneer and cook for more 5 minutes.

6. Garnish with coriander leaves.
