Roasted Squash

Description

Roasted squash is an Autumnal masterpiece of sweet fudgey squash, tart cabbage and onions and cream.

Ingredients

Small Squashes

3

Butter

30g

Thyme Leaves

1 tsp

Large Onions

2

Olive Oil

3 tbsp

Small Bunch Parsley

1

Cabbage

250g

Double Cream

150ml

Grated Cheddar Cheese

125g

Method

1. Set the oven at 200°C. Cut the squashes in half and use a spoon to remove the seeds and fibres. Place the squash hollow side up in a roasting tin.

2. Then divide the butter and thyme between them. Season with salt and black pepper and bake the squashes for 45 minutes to an hour until the flesh is soft and giving.

3. While the squashes bake, peel and finely slice the onions. Warm the oil in a deep pan, over a moderate heat, add the onions and cook until pale gold and soft.

4. Add the cabbage into the pan with the onions.

5. Roughly chop the parsley, then stir into the softened onions.

6. Remove from the heat and add the cream and cheese.
