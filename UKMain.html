Chicken with Lemon and Mint

Description

Chicken is a staple in the UK and what better way than to spice it up!

Ingredients

Romano Peppers

4

Orange or Yellow Peppers

2

Cherry Tomatoes

400g

Garlic Cloves

4

Chicken Thighs

6

Olive Oil

6 tbsp

Preserved Lemon

1

Mint Leaves

A Small Handfull

Method

1. Slice the romano peppers in half lengthways, remove any seeds, then cut each half into two short lengths. Do the same with the orange or yellow peppers, then put all of them in a roasting tin.

2.Add the cherry tomatoes, left whole. Set the oven at 200°C.

3.Peel the garlic cloves, flatten them with a knife and add them to the roasting tin. Place the chicken thighs, skin side up, among the peppers, pour over the olive oil and season well with salt and black pepper.

4. Bake for 50 minutes to an hour, until the chicken is golden brown and crisp and the peppers are soft.

5.Halve the preserved lemon and scrape out and discard the flesh. Chop the lemon shells into small pieces, then add to the juices in the roasting tin and continue cooking for a couple of minutes.

6. Put the chicken, tomatoes and peppers onto plates, then chop the mint leaves and stir them into the juices in the roasting tin. Spoon the juices over the chicken and peppers and serve.
