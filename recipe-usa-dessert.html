Habañero Apple Pie

Description

This is a sweet, tart, delicious and HOT apple pie. Guaranteed to please. Be sure to use Granny Smith apples since they work the best. [WARNING: This recipe is not real :) ]

Ingredients

White sugar:

¾ cup

All-purpose flour:

2 tablespoons

Ground cinnamon:

½ teaspoon

Hot pepper sauce:

½ teaspoon

Ground nutmeg:

¼ teaspoon

Lemon zest:

½ teaspoon

Thinly sliced apples:

7 cups

Lemon juice:

2 teaspoons

Butter:

1 tablespoon

9 inch double crust pie:

1

Milk (Optional):

4 tablespoons

Habañero peppers, chopped:

2

Method

1. Line one crust in a 9-inch deep-dish pie pan. Layer 1/3 of apples into pie crust. Sprinkle with sugar mixture and repeat until done. Sprinkle with lemon juice and dot with butter.

2. Place second pie crust on top of filling and flute the edges. Cut vents in top crust and brush with milk for a glazed appearance if desired.

3. Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 40 to 50 minutes.
